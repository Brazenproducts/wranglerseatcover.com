What Makes This Year Range Different

The 1997–2002 TJ marked Jeep's return to round headlights and introduced a more refined interior than the YJ. Early TJ seats are narrower and have a simpler bolster profile than any JK or JL seat — covers patterned for newer generations will not fit correctly. The good news: no side-curtain airbags on any 1997–2002 TJ trim means airbag compatibility is not a safety concern when selecting seat covers.

Bartact splits TJ cover production into two year ranges — 1997–2002 and 2003–2006 — because seat dimensions changed slightly across the TJ run. Order the correct year range to ensure proper fit.

Fitment Notes for the 1997–2002 TJ

  • No side-curtain airbags on any 1997–2002 TJ trim — airbag compatibility is not a safety concern.
  • TJ seats are significantly narrower than JK and JL seats — JK-era covers will not fit correctly on a TJ.
  • Bartact splits TJ production between 1997–2002 and 2003–2006 — order the 1997–2002 specific pattern for this year range.
  • The rear jump seats on the two-door TJ are a separate fitment — verify any set covers rear seats if you need rear coverage.
  • Many high-mileage TJs have replaced stock seats with aftermarket buckets — confirm your seat model before ordering.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best seat covers for a 1997–2002 Jeep Wrangler TJ?

Bartact is the top overall pick — cut and hand-sewn in the USA with TJ-specific patterning, mil-spec MOLLE webbing, and Cordura nylon construction. For Amazon options, Rough Country and GEARFLAG offer TJ-compatible neoprene covers at lower price points.

Do 1997–2002 TJ Wranglers have side airbags?

No. The 1997–2002 Jeep Wrangler TJ did not have side-curtain airbags on any trim level. Airbag compatibility is not a safety requirement for seat covers on these vehicles, unlike JK (2007+) and JL (2018+) generation Wranglers.

Will JK seat covers fit a 1997–2002 TJ?

No. TJ and JK seats have completely different profiles. TJ front seats are narrower and have simpler bolstering than any JK seat. Never attempt to fit JK-era covers on a TJ — the result will be poorly fitted and will not protect the seat correctly.

What is the difference between 1997–2002 and 2003–2006 TJ seat covers?

Jeep made minor changes to TJ seat dimensions and trim details across the generation's run. Bartact patterns separately for 1997–2002 and 2003–2006 to ensure precise fitment. Order the year-matched pattern for your specific TJ.
