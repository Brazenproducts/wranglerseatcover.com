What Makes This Year Range Different

Jeep refreshed the JK interior in 2011 with updated trim panels, revised seat shapes, and expanded standard equipment. This means covers sized specifically for the 2007–2010 interior may not fit the 2011+ seats correctly — always verify the listed year range in any product you consider purchasing.

Side-curtain airbags became more widely available across trim levels starting with the 2011 refresh. Verify your specific trim's airbag equipment via the door jamb sticker before purchasing seat covers. Bartact's SAB-compatible seam construction handles both airbag and non-airbag trims safely.

Fitment Notes for the 2011–2012 JK/JKU

  • The 2011 interior refresh means some 2007–2010 JK covers may not fit correctly — verify year range compatibility.
  • Side airbags more widely standard in 2011–2012 vs. earlier JK trims — always check your specific trim's equipment.
  • Two-door JK and four-door JKU front seats are the same. Rear seats are completely different — specify body style for rear coverage.
  • Bartact's SAB-compatible seam construction handles both airbag and non-airbag trims safely.
  • JKU rear seats fold and tumble — verify installed covers do not restrict this function before driving.
