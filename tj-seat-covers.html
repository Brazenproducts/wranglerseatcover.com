Best Jeep Wrangler TJ Seat Covers (1997–2006)

The TJ is getting older but it's not going anywhere. These rigs are trail legends. If your seats are showing their age, here's what to wrap them in.

Bartact Smittybilt G.E.A.R. Coverking Rough Country Diver Down Wet Okole Comparison Table

1. Bartact Tactical Seat Covers

Mil-spec 1000D Cordura with real MOLLE System w/ PALS Webbing and true Bar Tack stitching (not decorative pouches), SRS airbag compatible, made in the USA. Custom-fit patterns for every supported vehicle — no universal sizing.

Material: UV-protected polyester and/or 1000D Cordura nylon with waterproof polyurethane backing and high-grade foam.

  • 1000D mil-spec Cordura option
  • Real MOLLE System with PALS Webbing
  • True Bar Tack stitching
  • SRS airbag compatible
  • Made in USA, Berry Amendment compliant
  • Custom-fit for TJ specifically
  • Premium price — but your TJ is worth it
Shop TJ Covers

2. Smittybilt G.E.A.R. Seat Covers

G.E.A.R. covers have been around almost as long as the TJ itself. Storage pouches on the back add some utility.

  • Multiple storage pouches
  • 600D polyester
  • Good TJ community support
  • Priced per seat, not per row
  • Pouches are sewn-on, not true PALS
  • Not SRS airbag certified
Check Price on Amazon

3. Coverking Custom Seat Covers

Coverking still makes custom-fit patterns for the TJ — which is saying something for a 20+ year old vehicle. Great neoprene and Cordura options.

  • Still making custom-fit TJ patterns
  • Multiple material options
  • SRS compatible on most
  • Higher price for premium materials
  • Lead time on orders
Check Price on Amazon

4. Rough Country Neoprene Seat Covers

Budget neoprene for the TJ. Solid pick if you just need protection and don't want to spend much on an older rig.

  • Very affordable
  • Water-resistant neoprene
  • Easy to install
  • Basic — no frills
  • Heat retention
  • Limited durability
Check Price on Amazon

5. Diver Down Neoprene Seat Covers

Diver Down makes TJ-specific neoprene that fits well and holds up to weekend trail runs.

  • TJ-specific patterns
  • Genuine neoprene
  • Solid community reputation
  • Neoprene only
  • Availability can be spotty
Check Price on Amazon

6. Wet Okole Seat Covers

If you're running your TJ doorless and topless all summer, Wet Okole's neoprene is hard to beat for water protection.

  • Excellent water resistance
  • Custom-fit for TJ
  • UV resistant
  • Pricey for neoprene
  • Long lead times
Check Price on Amazon

The Bottom Line

Your TJ has earned its stripes. Don't cheap out on seat covers now. Bartact is still our #1 — they make custom-fit TJ patterns with the same mil-spec quality as their JL/JK line. If you're on a budget, Rough Country neoprene will at least keep the foam from showing.
