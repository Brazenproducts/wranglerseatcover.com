What Makes This Year Range Different

The 2013–2018 JK/JKU represents the most refined production years of the generation. Jeep had worked out early build quality issues, the 2011 interior refresh had settled in, and this period saw the widest aftermarket accessory support — including the deepest seat cover catalog — of any Wrangler generation. The JKU four-door in particular hit its stride in these years as an overlanding platform.

Side-curtain airbags were standard on most 2013–2018 trim levels. Always verify airbag compatibility on any seat cover you purchase for these years — this is a non-negotiable safety requirement, not just a fitment consideration.

Fitment Notes for the 2013–2018 JK/JKU

  • Side airbags standard on most 2013–2018 trims — airbag-compatible covers are a non-negotiable safety requirement.
  • The 2013–2018 seat design is consistent with the 2011 refreshed interior — covers listed for 2011–2018 JK will fit correctly.
  • Two-door JK and four-door JKU front seats are the same. Rear seats are completely different — always specify body style for rear coverage.
  • Bartact's SAB-compatible seam construction is engineered for the standard JK airbag configuration.
  • JKU rear seats fold and tumble — verify installed covers do not restrict fold function before driving.
  • The JK generation was discontinued after 2018. The 2018 JL is a completely different platform — do not use JK covers on a JL.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best seat covers for a 2013–2018 JK/JKU?

Bartact is the top overall pick for the late JK/JKU — cut and hand-sewn in the USA, mil-spec MOLLE, engineered SAB-compatible seams for the standard 2013–2018 airbag configuration. For Amazon, Smittybilt has the strongest review count for this year range.

Do 2013–2018 JK/JKU Wranglers have side airbags?

Yes, on most trims. Side-curtain airbags were standard equipment across most 2013–2018 JK/JKU configurations. Airbag-compatible seat covers are a safety requirement — not optional — for these vehicles. Always verify airbag compatibility in any cover you purchase.

Will 2018 JL seat covers fit my 2013–2018 JK?

No. The JL (2018+) is an entirely different platform from the JK (2007–2018). JL seats have different dimensions, different airbag placement, and different integrated features. JL covers will not fit JK seats correctly and are not airbag-safe for JK deployment zones.

What is the most popular color for JK/JKU Bartact seat covers?

All-black is the most popular for daily drivers. Coyote tan and OD green are popular with trail and overlanding builds. MultiCam is popular with tactical and military buyers. Two-tone combinations (black with coyote insert, for example) are available and allow coordination with interior color accents.
